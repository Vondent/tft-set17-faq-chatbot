# TFT Patch 17.1 — Balance Changes
Source: https://teamfighttactics.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/news/game-updates/teamfight-tactics-patch-17-1/

## Augments — Removed

- Golden Dragon Removed [REMOVED]
- Just Hit Removed [REMOVED]
- Max Build Removed [REMOVED]
