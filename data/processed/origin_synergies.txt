# TFT Set 17 — Origin Synergies

## Unique Traits (Single Champion, No Breakpoints)

Trait: Bulwark
Type: Origin (Unique — Shen)
Description: Summon a placeable relic. At the start of combat, it grants adjacent allies a 10% max Health shield and 10% Attack Speed.

---

Trait: Dark Lady
Type: Origin (Unique — Morgana)
Description: Allies take 5% less damage from abilities, increased to 10% while Morgana is in Dark Form.

---

Trait: Doomer
Type: Origin (Unique — Vex)
Description: Combat Start: Mark all enemies with Doom.

---

Trait: Eradicator
Type: Origin (Unique — Jhin)
Description: Enemies have 10% less Armor and Magic Resist.

---

Trait: Factory New
Type: Origin (Unique — Graves)
Description: After participating in combat, open an armory to purchase a permanent upgrade for your strongest Graves. Every 3 upgrades, future upgrades will take an additional round.

---

Trait: Oracle
Type: Origin (Unique — Tahm Kench)
Description: Every 3 rounds, Tahm Kench grants a reward.

## Standard Traits

Trait: Redeemer
Type: Origin
Description: For each non-unique trait you have active, your team gains bonus Attack Speed and Armor and Magic Resist.
Breakpoints: 1
  (1): 2%/3%/3% Attack Speed and 2/2.5/3 Armor and Magic Resist (scales with Redeemer star level)

Trait: Anima
Type: Origin
Description: After losing a player combat, gain 20 Tech plus additional Tech equal to 5 times the length of your loss streak. Additionally, gain 2 Tech per Anima takedown. Each time Animas get 100 Tech, they prototype new Anima Weapons — take them now or save Tech for more powerful weapons later.
Breakpoints: 3/6
  (3): Start Researching! (begin prototyping Anima Weapons)
  (6): After winning a player combat, gain loot!

## Stargazer
Stargazers chart a different constellation every game, which determines their bonus effects and playstyle. At 3 Stargazers, hexes on the board are revealed and empowered — more hexes reveal at each player level.

### The Altar
When ANY champion dies, they are sacrificed to the Altar. Allies in empowered hexes gain 8% Health and 8% Attack Speed. After 60 sacrifices, Stargazers in empowered hexes gain an additional 20% bonus and 18% bonus.
Breakpoints: 3
  (3): Active — sacrifice mechanic begins (Sacrifices tracked toward 60)

---

### The Boar
Gain gold after winning player combat. Allies in empowered hexes gain 8% Health, Attack Damage, and Ability Power. Stargazers gain more.
Breakpoints: 3/4/5/6
  (3): 1 gold, 2% Health, 10% Attack Damage and Ability Power
  (4): 2 gold, 8% Health, 15% Attack Damage and Ability Power
  (5): 4 gold, 15% Health, 25% Attack Damage and Ability Power
  (6): 6 gold, 22% Health, 30% Attack Damage and Ability Power

---

### The Fountain
Allies in empowered hexes gain 1 Mana Regen. Stargazers in empowered hexes gain more Mana Regen and heal the lowest Health ally with their abilities.
Breakpoints: 3/5
  (3): 1 Mana Regen (team), 18% ability damage converted to healing
  (5): 5 Mana Regen (team), 25% ability damage converted to healing

---

### The Huntress
Combat Start: Mark the highest Health enemies. Allies in empowered hexes gain 15% Attack Speed. Stargazers in empowered hexes gain more Attack Speed and heal for 10% of their max Health when a marked enemy dies.
Breakpoints: 3/5/7
  (3): 10% Attack Speed for Stargazers, 3 marks
  (5): 30% Attack Speed for Stargazers, 5 marks
  (7): 45% Attack Speed for Stargazers, 7 marks

---

### The Medallion
Breakpoints: 3
  (3): Allies in empowered hexes gain 12% Damage Amp, which increases by 6.5% for each 3-star ally

---

### The Mountain
Every 5 player combats, gain a Stargazer Emblem. Stargazers in empowered hexes gain stacking bonuses as trait tier increases.
Breakpoints: 3/4/5/6/7/8
  (3): +12% Health
  (4): AND +12% Attack Damage and Ability Power
  (5): AND +12 Armor and Magic Resist
  (6): AND +12% Attack Speed
  (7): AND +6% Durability
  (8): AND increase all other bonuses by 10%

---

### The Serpent
Allies in empowered hexes gain 5% Durability. Stargazers in empowered hexes gain more Durability and poison enemies, repeating a portion of damage dealt as magic damage over 3 seconds.
Breakpoints: 3/5/7
  (3): 5% Durability for Stargazers, 25% poison damage
  (5): 10% Durability for Stargazers, 40% poison damage
  (7): 15% Durability for Stargazers, 60% poison damage
